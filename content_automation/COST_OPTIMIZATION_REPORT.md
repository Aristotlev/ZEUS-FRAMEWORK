# 🏛️ Zeus Framework - Media Generation Cost Optimization

## 💰 **Voice Generation: Fish Audio vs ElevenLabs**

### **Before (ElevenLabs):**
- **Pricing:** $22/month subscription (Creator plan)
- **Usage Model:** Monthly recurring regardless of usage
- **Character Limit:** 30,000 characters/month included
- **Overage:** $0.30/1,000 characters
- **Annual Cost:** $264/year minimum

### **After (Fish Audio):** ✅
- **Pricing:** $15 per 1 million characters
- **Usage Model:** Pay-as-you-go (no subscription)
- **Character Limit:** None (pay per use)
- **Cost Per Character:** $0.000015/character vs $0.00073/character (ElevenLabs)
- **Savings:** **98.8% cheaper per character!**

**Example: 100,000 characters monthly**
- ElevenLabs: $22/month (fixed) = $264/year
- Fish Audio: $1.50/month = $18/year
- **Annual Savings: $246 (93% less)**

- Scales to paid tiers only if you exceed free limits
